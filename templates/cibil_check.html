What is a CIBIL Score?

A CIBIL score is a 3-digit number ranging from 300 to 900 that represents your creditworthiness. A higher score means better loan eligibility and lower interest rates.

300–549 Poor
550–649 Fair
650–749 Good ✓
750–900 Excellent ✓
